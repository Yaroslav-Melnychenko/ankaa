Details

The Ankaa Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Perfecting Serum is designed with a unique formula to focus on visibly smoothing, lifting and tightening all facial zones while nourishing the skin with potent anti-aging compounds, we selected powerful botanical ingredients. It includes high doses of powerful extracts of botanical ingredients with the most advanced laboratory tested skincare technology. For this serum, we source botanical ingredients that are native to some of the most extreme environments on earth, including flora from high mountain regions and the deep seas; this is part of our exclusive LS-NT Complex. The formulation also includes a patent pending complex we developed, is paraben and fragrance free, and is clinically, dermatologically, and allergy tested.

Ingredients

For the Ankaa Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Perfecting Serum, we selected extracts from powerful botanical ingredients, particularly:

  1. Swertia Chirata – a flowering plant found in the high Himalayan Mountains that is known for its skin regeneration and anti-wrinkle qualities.
  2. Caulerpa Lentillifera – a green algae native to the Indian and Pacific Oceans to promote the smoothing and firming of the skin.

Additional key ingredients include: Bellis Perennis (Daisy) Flower extract to promote the even pigmentation of skin spots; Bidens Pilosa extract functioning as a natural bioretinol for skin firming and combatting wrinkles; Pumpkin Seed Oil as a potent source of anti-oxidants and to aid in skin tone and smoothing; Mango Seed Butter to provide vitamins to the skin and increase hydration; Niacinamide to promote even skin tone and minimize wrinkles; and, Xylitylglucoside and Xylitol to help with moisturizing and skin smoothing.

Formulated without parabens and fragrances.

Ingredient listing: water; glycerin; bellis perennis (daisy) flower extract; maltodextrin; mangifera indica (mango) seed butter; cucurbita pepo (pumpkin) seed oil; caulerpa lentillerfera extract; hydrolyzed rice protein; dipalmitoyl hydroxyproline; borago officinalis seed oil; polyacrylate crosspolymer-6; polysorbate 60; swertia chirata extract; tetrahexydelcyl ascorbate; niaciamide; bidens pilosa extract; elaeis guineensis (palm) oil; Gossypium herbaceum (cotton) seed oil; linum usitissimum (linseed) oil; xylitylglucoside; anhydroxylitol; xylitol; phenoxyethanol; silica; tocopheryl acetate; ethylhexylglycerin; sodium PCA; and, disodium EDTA.

How to use

To get optimal benefits from this special cream, we recommend using it twice a day, in the morning and evening. Apply after cleansing and before use of our Ultra-Hydrating Moisturizer.
