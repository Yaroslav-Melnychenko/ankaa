Frequently asked questions

Returning customer?

Yes, you can order our products on the phone by contacting our customer service representatives. They will also answer any queries you might have and help you understand the ordering process better.

Have a coupon?

Yes, we also ship internationally to various destinations. The process of placing an order from your country is simple. Use the shopping cart to make your purchases and choose your country from the list provided. If the name of your country does not figure in the list, it means we are currently not shipping there.

How do I track my package?

Tracking an order placed with us is simple. When you have confirmed your order, you will receive a message on the email address registered with us which will also have a tracking code/number. This code can be used to track your package. The tracking information can also be seen on the My Account page along with your purchase details.

I have a promotional code. How do I enter it?

Promotional codes associated with our products help you save money on purchases. Make sure you apply the code in the space provided for the same at checkout. The system will automatically apply the discount on the final invoice.

How do I know my order has been completed successfully?

When an order placed with us is completed successfully, you will get a message on your registered mobile number and your registered email address. You will also be directed to a page where detailed information about the order is provided.

When will my order be shipped?

We offer multiple shipping options to our customers. The time of shipping out of your order would depend on the shipping mode you have chosen for your purchase. When an order is shipped out, we send out a notification message on the registered email and mobile number of our customer/s. Generally, orders are shipped out within 24 to 48 hours of confirmation.

Do you give away any samples?

Currently, we do not have any facility for providing free samples to our customers. We follow this practice because our product has to be used for a specific time frame for results to be visible. The sample content will most probably not deliver the desired results. Our guarantee of 100% satisfaction is proof of the high level of confidence we have in our product.

Is the information I enter on your website safe and secure?

We follow a stringent policy of not sharing information provided by our customers with any third party. All details of the transaction remain safe and secure with us. You can refer to our privacy policy for more details on how we keep your information safe with us.
