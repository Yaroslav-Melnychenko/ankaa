Bidens Pilosa

This extract is used to produce visibly younger, firm, and lustrous skin, not only for helping to reduce wrinkles and improve skin elasticity, but to also produce a barrier of protection and hydration. In traditional folk medicine Bidens Pilosa was used to treat a wide range of ailments internally and externally; on the skin it was used in treating cuts, burns, wounds and snake bites, while at the same time being considered soothing and gentle enough for use in bathing babies and children. Contemporary studies reveal it to have a rich phytochemical composition with anti-inflammatory properties and rich in antioxidants. It is used to help:

  • Reduce deep wrinkles, smooth skin and increase elasticity of skin.
  • Operate as a natural bioretinol by increasing retinoid receptors and stimulate synthesis of dermal proteins and growth factors.
  • Decrease free radicals through its powerful antioxidants.
  • Combat effects of aging on skin through its anti-inflammatory properties.
In test using bidens pilosa, after 28 days of use, wrinkle depth reduction was demonstrated. Concentrations and results may vary with Ankaa skin care products.
Originating in moist and warm tropical regions of the Americas, Bidens Pilosa is a fast growing, strong, flowering plant.
Bidens Pilosa is found in both Ankaa’s Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Ultra-Hydrating Moisturizer and Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Perfecting Serum.
