Our story

We have always been passionate about health and beauty and are aware of the incredible ability of nature to provide solutions to problems related to these subjects. We have combined the power of nature and path-breaking solutions offered by science to create a line of skincare products that is significantly different from what is being offered by today’s skin and beauty industry.

The Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Ultra-Hydrating Moisturizer and Smooth & Lift Time-Resist Perfecting Serum are highly advanced formulations created specifically to help women manage their aging skin problems better. They have been created after years of extensive research conducted in state-of-the-art labs by the world’s most renowned specialists and experts from the skin care industry.

We have formulated these unique skincare solutions using high doses of powerful extracts of botanical ingredients with the help of the most advanced skincare technology.

We use high grade botanical ingredients that are native to some of the most extreme environments on earth. The flora we use in our brands is sourced from high Himalayan Mountains and the nutrients-rich deep seas.

Our offerings include our exclusive formulae such as the LS-NT Complex and the patent pending Complex. Our high-performing plant-powered formula works deep within the skin’s surface to provide all-day hydration, visibly lift, firm, and tighten all facial zones, and reduce the appearance of large pores, wrinkles, and fine lines.

Ankaa skin care line restores youthful vitality and radiance, infusing the skin with a new vibrancy and glow.
